{% include 'search.html'%} Elib Online eBook Delivery/Library System (Policy)
1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to outline the guidelines and procedures for the use of the eLib Online eBook Delivery/Library System.

2. Access and Membership
  • Access to the eLib system is restricted to registered members only.
  • Membership is open to individuals who meet the eligibility criteria set forth by the eLib administration.
  • Users must provide accurate and current information during the registration process.
  • Membership may be revoked or suspended for violations of the eLib usage policy.
3. Usage Guidelines
  • Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their login credentials and must not share them with others.
  • Users must respect copyright laws and licensing agreements when accessing and downloading eBooks from the eLib system.
  • The eLib system is for personal and educational use only. Commercial use of eBooks obtained through the system is prohibited.
  • Users must adhere to all applicable laws and regulations while using the eLib system.
  • Any attempt to modify or disrupt the eLib system is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.
4. Privacy and Data Security
  • The eLib system collects and stores personal information in accordance with its privacy policy.
  • Users' personal information will not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law or legal process.
  • The eLib system employs security measures to protect users' personal information and prevent unauthorized access.
5. Content Management
  • The eLib administration reserves the right to add, remove, or modify eBooks available through the system.
  • Users may request specific titles to be added to the eLib collection, subject to approval by the administration.
  • Inappropriate or offensive content will not be tolerated and will be promptly removed from the eLib system.
6. Conduct and Etiquette
  • Users are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and professional manner while using the eLib system.
  • Any form of harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated and may result in suspension or termination of membership.
  • Users must adhere to the eLib community guidelines and report any violations to the administration.
7. Enforcement
  • The eLib administration reserves the right to enforce this policy and take appropriate action against users who violate its terms.
  • Violations of this policy may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent expulsion from the eLib system.
  • Users have the right to appeal disciplinary actions taken against them by contacting the eLib administration.
8. Policy Review
  • This policy will be reviewed periodically and updated as necessary to ensure its effectiveness and compliance with relevant laws and regulations.
  • Users will be notified of any changes to the policy via email or through the eLib system.
